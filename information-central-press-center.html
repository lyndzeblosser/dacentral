Detroit Police Department Moves to Verified Response – 2012 Update
posted on November 29, 2012 by admin, Article courtesy of Homeland Security Newswire

Please click on the title above for some important news about how the Detroit Police Department has changed their policy on responding to alarms to one that is called "Verified Response". This policy requires that the police must receive an audio or visual confirmation that a crime is taking place before responding to the alarm. What does this mean for Detroit businesses and homeowners? It might be time to install or update a security system to include components that send audio or visual clips along with the alarm notification to the monitoring company so that police will be dispatched for a real emergency!
