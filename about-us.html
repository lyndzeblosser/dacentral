About Us – Who is D/A Central

D/A Central: Designed to Protect You

We are a systems integrator company that designs security systems and access control systems that best suit your company and individual needs. Combining the best security products in the industry, we provide intelligent security solutions to public and private corporations and institutions, as well as residential owners.

Our dedication to quality, ongoing industry research and involvement in the security business ensure that we will best serve you as your single-source provider for your system design, integration, and support. With more than 55 years of experience and leadership in the security industry, you can be assured that we will provide an integrated system that best fits your unique security needs.

Our Vision and Mission

To be the premier security systems integrator and service provider that is sought out to provide high value solutions and services that create safe environments to corporations and institutions, allowing clients to operate productively and comfortably.

To serve our clients with integrity, excellence, and intelligence by facilitating their ability to provide protection, safety, and security for their people, facilities, property, and knowledge base through delivery of the best, most effective, and highest value security services and solutions.

Demographics

We are well-positioned geographically to serve Michigan, Northern Ohio, and Northern Indiana. In addition to these local areas, we also service international companies, either directly or through one of our Partnership Affiliates. D/A is a charter member of SecurityNet, and is also a member of PSA and has active memberships in the American Society for Industrial Security (ASIS), Burglar and Fire Association of Michigan (BFAM), International Association for Health Care Security and Safety (IAHSS) and American Institute of Architects (AIA).

Our offices are throughout Michigan, although our main office is located in Oak Park. Our satellite sales and service distribution branches are in Grand Rapids and Mid Michigan. These employees function in management, sales, administration, operations, engineering, documentation and installation. Approximately 75% of the employees are directly involved in service and client support.

Our History

D/A Central, Inc. has earned its clients respect and trust by dedication and personal investment in research and understanding of advanced security solutions since 1957. We are an Integrated Intelligent Security Solutions provider that has always been an industry leader in providing innovative technology for the safety and security of your people, property and assets. We were originally known as Regiscope of Michigan. In the early 70's our name was changed to D/A Central, Inc. as our relationship grew with D/A General of Massachusetts. Current president and owner, P. David Shelton started with the company in 1971 and then acquired the growing business in 1984.

Full service support capabilities are available throughout Michigan radiating from Detroit, Grand Rapids and the Tri-City/Flint locations. Security Distributing and Marketing (SDM) has given D/A Central, Inc. national recognition by ranking us in the top 100 Security System Integrators in the United States since 1996.

Our original solutions included specializing in film surveillance and processing, combined with consultation, design and installation. As technology advanced, engineered systems included CCTV, intrusion detection and monitoring systems. Other services included access control, digital networked video management, storage and service solutions.

Today, we have the ability to integrate IT security with physical security systems, for improved value. This allows clients unparalleled access to experts and solutions that protect corporate assets, improves safety, and more effectively uses resources.

Whether you are looking to engineer and install a new system, upgrade or expand your current security system, or simply take advantage of our consulting services, we will work with you to determine the best solution to protect your company.
